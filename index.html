Welcome to a showcase my lighting collection collection;

I've been interested in lighting, specifically fluorescent lights since I was a little boy with some of my earliest memories of me pointing out dead lights in preschool, watching failing f40t12's in an arcade flicker and an f40t12 going EOL at my grandparent's house.

Some of my first fixtures were my American Fluorescent Company model ms104 which runs one f4t5, two 1985 undercabinet fixtures from my uncle and my shoplight which was used as a growlight by my neighbor for around 30 years.
